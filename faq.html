Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about earning, tasks, and payouts on ZimFaucet.

What is the minimum withdrawal amount?

The minimum amount for withdrawal is $0.50 USD.

What is the withdrawal fee?

We charge a 5% fee on all withdrawal amounts to cover processing costs and currency conversion.

How long does a withdrawal take?

Withdrawals are typically processed within 24-48 hours, depending on the chosen payment gateway.

Which payment gateways are supported?

We currently support Ecocash, OneMoney, and Inbux/InBucks for local transfers.

How does the referral program work?

You earn a 10% commission on all earnings generated by users who signed up using your unique referral link.

Is there a limit to how much I can earn per day?

There's no hard limit on daily earnings. Your potential earnings depend on the number of available ads and tasks, and how actively you participate.

Do I need a cryptocurrency wallet?

No! We handle all cryptocurrency aspects automatically. You earn in USD and receive payments in local currency through supported gateways.

What happens if an ad doesn't credit my account?

If you encounter issues with ad credits, please contact our support team with details of the ad and time viewed. We'll investigate and credit your account if verified.

Can I use multiple accounts?

No, multiple accounts are strictly prohibited. Each user is allowed only one account. Violation may result in permanent suspension of all accounts.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. Your personal information is encrypted and never shared with third parties without your consent. We comply with all applicable data protection regulations.
